A 'mentor' is someone who serves as a counselor or a guide. To have a mentor is an honor. Mentor could be anyone - our teacher, our parents, any senior schoolmate or a person in our neighbourhood. The one who teaches us how to live our life to monitor our ways, actions and to tell us how to get success in our life. They guide us about the basic routines, do's and don'ts and the various job responsibilities. Thus there is a great role of mentor in our lives.
